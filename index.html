ABOUT:

Wroht is a metal band hailing from Oakland, California, that draws inspiration from old-school death metal while incorporating modern elements for a sound that is both crushing and immersive. Their debut EP "Trails of Blood" garnered attention with impressive musicianship and dynamic songwriting, setting the stage for their debut album “Worship Rot” in 2018.

Wroht's debut album "Worship Rot" was praised as “a galloping death feast… Wroht has a killer death metal debut with this one” by Path to Babylon. Their complex riffs, thunderous drums, and guttural vocals create a sonic experience that is both intense and powerful. While their themes may touch on darkness and despair, Wroht's music is also empowering and exhilarating, inspiring fans to headbang and let loose.

After an indefinite hiatus from 2018 to 2022, Wroht is back with renewed vigor. Wroht's live performances are known for their intensity, delivering a captivating show that leaves audiences in awe. The band's upcoming follow-up album, set for release in 2024, promises to build upon their established sound while progressing their music even further. With a sound that is both classic and forward-thinking, Wroht is poised to make their mark on the metal scene with their powerful music. As the band continues to grow and evolve, they cement their place as one of the most exciting underground bands in the genre, delivering a must-listen experience for any metal fan.
